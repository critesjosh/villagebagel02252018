About Us

We are East Coast transplants living in the mountains whose happiest memories come from the food traditions of our childhoods in the Northeast, particularly the local bakery and its warm, Saturday-morning-bagels. We are passionate about food made the old fashioned way, and the places where people gather with a shared happiness to eat the foods of their youth. We hope to be your neighborhood bagel shop. For now, we are popping up all around the Vail Valley, shmearing happiness one hand-rolled bagel at a time. Follow us on Facebook to see where you can find Village Bagel pop up next!
